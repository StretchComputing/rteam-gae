rTeam Features
rTeam allows team members and fans to do a lot ...
  • keep score
  • take attendance
  • send messages and polls
  • manage team roster and schedules
  • participate as members using the app, email or text messages
  • synch with your phone calendar
  • post text, photos and videos to a live activity feed
  • map the location of any of your games, practices or events
but the real game changer is what rTeam does for you
  • automatically notifying the team of schedule updates
  • handling message confirmations and poll responses
  • archiving old inbox messages
  • posting team activity to Twitter
  • sending game summary messages to all team members and fans
  • always keeping you informed of 'What's Happening' right now!
