rTeam Frequently Asked Questions
What is rTeam?
rTeam is a mobile app that handles all aspects of communication for sports teams. It is intended for youth sports, high school teams, college intramurals and adult leagues. The goal of rTeam is allow teams to enjoy faster, more effective communications by allowing team members to send and receive messages in the way that is most natural and convenient to them.
Does rTeam only run on the iPhone?
Yes. Android, iPad and a web versions are currently being developed and we will make them available as soon as we can.
Does everyone need a iPhone to use rTeam?
No, definitely not! The coach or team coordinator currently needs to have an iPhone to create the team and get the ball rolling. Members can participate in a number of ways including: the rTeam app, email, text messaging or Twitter.
Will rTeam always be free?
Yes. Currently, rTeam is a free download from the iTunes App Store. rTeam does display iAds as a source of revenue. In the future, we may provide an option for users to buy out of the ads if they so choose.
What does 'powered by the cloud' mean?
If you haven't heard this term before, you will start hearing it more. Cloud computing is what powers all internet services today like Google, Amazon, etc. What it means to our users is that rTeam is not a single user app that simply runs on the phone. Rather, it is a multi-user app that makes information available to everyone, instantly. For example, if one user updates a game score on their phone, that score is available to all team members and fans right away.
